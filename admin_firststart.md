First connection
==================

When connecting to the Platform for the first time, perform the following steps:

\#. After installation is complete, enter the address of the installed Platform obtained after going through :ref:`installation <wizard>` in the internet browser. The Platform connection window will appear.

	.. thumbnail:: ../_static/media/login_main.png
		:width: 350
		:align: center

\#. Enter the email address provided during the installation to the personnel in charge of installing the Platform, in the text field 'Email' ('Email address').

\#. Enter the initial password received from the personnel in charge of installing the Platform in the text field 'Password'. This password needs to be changed after the first connection.

\#. Click 'Login' ('Connect'). 'New password' window will appear:

	.. thumbnail:: ../_static/media/image111.png
		:width: 350
		:align: center

\#. Enter a new password in the text field (digit 1 in the picture above). The new user password shall comply with the following requirements:

	The password shall consist of at least 8 characters.
	The password shall consist of Latin letters (A-z) and Arabic numerals (0-9).
	The alphabetical part of the password shall contain both lowercase and uppercase letters.
	* the password should not contain simple combinations, commonly known abbreviations, or consecutive characters. If the requirements are not met, an error will appear when saving the password.

		.. thumbnail:: ../_static/media/password_wellkonwn.png
			:width: 350
			:align: center

\#. Confirm the new password in the text field (digit 2 in the picture above).

\#. Click 'Change password'. Password will be changed and you will automatically logout from the Platform. The Platform connection window will appear.

	.. thumbnail:: ../_static/media/login_main.png
		:width: 350
		:align: center

\#. Enter your email address in the 'Email' field.
