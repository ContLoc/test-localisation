Information Tab
--------------------

The key information on the user is displayed in this tab. You can also manage the group settings here.

Editing the group ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To edit group data, click 'Edit'.

.. thumbnail:: ../\_static/media/edit\_group.png

In the modal window, edit the name and description of the group and press 'Save'.

.. thumbnail:: ../\_static/media/edit\_group\_1.png :width: 300 :align: center

Deleting the group ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To delete the group, click 'Delete Group'.

.. thumbnail:: ../\_static/media/delete\_group.png

In the modal window, enter 'delete' and click 'Delete Group'.

	.. thumbnail:: ../_static/media/delete_group_list.png
		:width: 300
		:align: center

'Workspaces' tab
------------------------------

All workspaces of the tenant are displayed on this tab.

Check 'View' and 'Edit' boxed for those workspaces to which group users will get access.

Workspaces can be filtered by privileges: all rights granted, viewing rights granted, or no rights granted.

.. thumbnail:: ../\_static/media/ws\_groups.png
