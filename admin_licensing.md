Licensing
==============

.. attention::

	Page with Platform License is only available to users with the role of System Owner.

The Platform is installed with a built-in license for 30 days, for 128 CPU cores. This license can be used for pilot projects in development and testing environments.

Select 'System Settings' -> 'License' tenants page in the left panel to open the page with the Platform license.

.. thumbnail:: ../\_static/media/goto\_liecense.png

Let 's go through the information on this page.

.. thumbnail:: ../\_static/media/current\_license.png

1. 'Current license' - information on current license of the user.
2. 'Modules' - information on licenses for additional modules of the Platform.
3. 'LICENSE RENEWAL INSTRUCTIONS' - to renew the license, follow the instructions on the page.
